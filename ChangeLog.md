## [1.0.0] - Released - May 3, 2026

- **Comprehensive study guide** (`LEETCODE_STUDY_GUIDE.md`) with deep dives into algorithmic patterns
- **Project documentation suite**:
  - README.md with project overview and quick start
  - RoadMap.md with project status and future work
  - HowItWorks.md with architecture overview
